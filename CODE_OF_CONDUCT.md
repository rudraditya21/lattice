# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming and respectful community.

## Standards
- Be respectful, inclusive, and considerate in all interactions.
- Give and receive constructive feedback kindly.
- Focus on collaboration and learning.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Disruptive behavior, trolling, or sustained interruptions.

## Reporting
- Report incidents to the maintainers via the contact listed in `SECURITY.md`.
- Provide as much context as possible (who, when, where, what).

## Enforcement
- Maintainers may take actions including warnings, temporary or permanent bans.
- Decisions are made to maintain a safe, productive environment.
